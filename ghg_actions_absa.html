GHG Action

How Absa Is Reducing GHG Emissions

Absa is reducing greenhouse gas emissions by lowering energy demand, increasing renewable energy contribution, reducing avoidable diesel use, and strengthening emissions data.

What Absa Is Doing

Energy Efficiency

Reducing energy demand lowers emissions associated with electricity and fuel use.

Energy Efficiency Buildings
  • Improving building energy efficiency through operational and technical measures.
  • Optimising lighting, HVAC and building controls where feasible.
  • Reducing avoidable energy use across buildings and operations.

Renewable Energy

Increasing renewable electricity contribution helps reduce emissions intensity.

Renewables Electricity Intensity
  • Increasing renewable energy contribution where suitable options are available.
  • Exploring on-site generation and renewable electricity procurement pathways.
  • Linking renewable energy performance to GHG reduction outcomes.

Diesel Reduction

Reducing avoidable diesel use supports lower direct emissions.

Diesel Backup power Direct emissions
  • Reviewing generator usage patterns where operationally feasible.
  • Reducing unnecessary diesel consumption linked to backup power systems.
  • Improving efficiency of backup systems where diesel use remains necessary.

Emissions Data Tracking

Reliable emissions data supports better decisions and stronger performance management.

Data Tracking Reporting
  • Tracking emissions sources and trends over time.
  • Improving visibility of the drivers behind emissions performance.
  • Supporting reporting against the 2030 GHG reduction target.
Looking Ahead

Integrated Emissions Reduction

GHG reduction depends on coordinated action across energy use, renewable energy, diesel use, travel and operational data.

  • Strengthening the link between performance data and action planning.
  • Prioritising actions that reduce both energy demand and emissions.
  • Improving visibility of progress against the 2030 emissions target.
Why this matters: Reducing GHG emissions supports climate targets, improves operational resilience, and links environmental performance directly to energy and resource management.
