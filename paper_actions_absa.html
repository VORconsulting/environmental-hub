Paper Action

How Absa Is Improving Paper Performance

Absa is reducing paper use through digital ways of working, improved print control, and better visibility of paper consumption across operations.

What Absa Is Doing

Digital Workflows

Reducing reliance on paper by moving more processes into digital channels.

Digital Approvals Records
  • Supporting digital review, approval and record-keeping processes.
  • Reducing paper-based workflows where practical.
  • Encouraging electronic sharing instead of printed circulation.

Print Management

Improving control over printing to reduce unnecessary paper consumption.

Printing Controls Usage
  • Reducing unnecessary printing and duplication.
  • Encouraging more efficient print settings where printing is needed.
  • Improving awareness of print volumes and paper use.

Paper Recycling

Supporting responsible recovery of paper that is still used.

Recycling Recovery Waste
  • Separating paper waste so it can enter recycling streams.
  • Supporting responsible paper recovery where systems are available.
  • Linking paper use reduction to broader waste diversion performance.

Paper Data Tracking

Improving visibility of paper use trends, sources and reduction opportunities.

Data Tracking Performance
  • Tracking paper use over time against the 2018 baseline.
  • Identifying where paper use can be reduced further.
  • Supporting performance reporting against reduction targets.
Looking Ahead

Maintaining Low Paper Use

Paper performance remains strongest when digital habits become normal operating practice.

  • Embedding paper-light ways of working into everyday processes.
  • Maintaining awareness even after strong reductions have been achieved.
  • Using performance data to avoid unnecessary increases over time.
Why this matters: Reducing paper use lowers resource consumption, reduces waste, supports digital efficiency, and strengthens overall environmental performance.
