Renewable Energy Action

How Absa Is Improving Renewable Energy Performance

Absa is improving renewable energy performance by increasing renewable electricity supply, improving energy data, and reducing total energy demand.

What Absa Is Doing

On-site Solar

Increasing renewable electricity generation where suitable sites and business cases allow.

Solar Buildings Generation
  • Assessing suitable sites for solar electricity generation.
  • Using on-site renewable electricity where technically and commercially feasible.
  • Supporting lower grid reliance where renewable generation is available.

Renewable Procurement

Exploring renewable electricity supply options beyond on-site generation.

Procurement Supply Renewables
  • Considering renewable electricity purchasing options where available.
  • Reviewing supply mechanisms that can increase renewable contribution.
  • Supporting progress towards the renewable energy target pathway.

Energy Demand Reduction

Reducing total energy demand makes renewable energy a larger share of the total.

Efficiency Demand Share
  • Reducing avoidable energy consumption across buildings and operations.
  • Improving efficiency so renewable energy has greater relative impact.
  • Linking renewable energy performance to overall energy management.

Renewable Energy Data

Improving visibility of renewable generation, procurement and contribution to total energy.

Data Tracking Reporting
  • Tracking renewable electricity generated or procured.
  • Calculating renewable energy as a share of total energy consumption.
  • Supporting reporting against the 2028 and 2030 renewable energy target.
Looking Ahead

Integrated Energy Transition

Renewable energy performance improves most when energy efficiency, procurement and emissions reduction are managed together.

  • Expanding renewable energy opportunities where practical.
  • Maintaining strong energy efficiency to reduce total demand.
  • Strengthening the link between renewable energy and GHG reduction.
Why this matters: Increasing renewable energy reduces reliance on higher-emission energy sources and strengthens progress toward emissions reduction targets.
