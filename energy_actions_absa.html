Energy Action

How Absa Is Improving Energy Performance

Absa is improving energy performance through targeted efficiency measures, better data visibility, and ongoing optimisation of how buildings and systems are used.

What Absa Is Doing

Building Energy Efficiency

Improving how buildings use energy through upgrades and system optimisation.

Building systems Lighting HVAC
  • Upgrading lighting to more efficient technologies.
  • Optimising heating, cooling, and ventilation systems.
  • Improving control through building management systems.

Energy Monitoring & Visibility

Making energy use visible to support better decisions.

Data Monitoring Reporting
  • Tracking energy use across sites and regions.
  • Identifying high-consumption areas.
  • Supporting performance tracking against targets.

Smarter Operations

Aligning energy use with how buildings are actually used.

Operations Efficiency Usage
  • Reducing energy use during low-demand periods.
  • Aligning systems with occupancy patterns.
  • Improving scheduling of building systems.

Reducing Diesel Use

Reducing reliance on diesel where feasible.

Diesel Backup power Optimisation
  • Reviewing generator usage patterns.
  • Reducing unnecessary diesel consumption.
  • Improving efficiency of backup systems.
Looking Ahead

Continuous Improvement

Energy performance continues to improve through ongoing action and refinement.

  • Expanding monitoring and data coverage.
  • Supporting country-level implementation.
  • Strengthening alignment between actions and performance.
Why this matters: Lower energy demand reduces costs, supports emissions reduction, and strengthens the impact of renewable energy initiatives.
