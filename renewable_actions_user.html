Renewable Energy Action

How You Can Support Renewable Energy Performance

Renewable energy is mainly delivered through Absa systems and procurement, but everyday energy choices can help renewable energy have a greater impact.

At Work

Reduce unnecessary energy use

Lower energy demand makes renewable energy a larger share of total energy use.

Energy use Demand Impact
  • Switch off lights, monitors and equipment that are not needed.
  • Use meeting rooms and shared spaces efficiently.
  • Avoid unnecessary use of personal heaters, fans or chargers.

Support efficient building use

Efficient buildings make renewable energy initiatives more effective.

Buildings HVAC Occupancy
  • Keep doors and windows closed when air conditioning is operating.
  • Release rooms that are no longer needed.
  • Report areas that are consistently overcooled, overheated or inefficient.

Understand the emissions link

Renewable electricity supports emissions reduction when it displaces higher-emission energy sources.

GHG Electricity Impact
  • Use energy efficiently so renewable sources go further.
  • Support internal energy-saving and awareness initiatives.
  • Recognise that energy efficiency and renewable energy work together.
Beyond the Workplace

Use energy more efficiently at home

Efficient energy habits support the broader shift to cleaner energy systems.

  • Switch off unused appliances and reduce standby power where possible.
  • Use efficient lighting and energy-saving settings on devices.
  • Reduce peak demand where practical by using major appliances efficiently.

Consider renewable options where practical

Personal choices can also support demand for cleaner energy over time.

  • Explore solar or renewable electricity options where suitable and affordable.
  • Choose energy-efficient appliances when replacing equipment.
  • Support suppliers and services that provide credible low-carbon options.
Why this matters: Renewable energy performance improves when clean supply increases and total energy demand is managed responsibly.
